Scientific laboratory of Fundamental Physics and Physics and Chemistry

PURPOSE

1. Solve problems of modern astrophysics and cosmology;
2. Carrying out ionospheric and atmospheric research.

RESULTS

Scientific results :
1. New astronomical and astrophysical objects are found and processes are identified;
2. Analysis of astrophysical objects on the basis of observational data.
Practical results:
1. A model of atmospheric moisture dynamics is developed;
2. Atmosphere and space climate are monitored online

TASKS

1. Digital modeling of astrophysical processes;
2. Processing of observational and experimental data and their application to astrophysical processes;
3. Monitoring of the basic parameters of the ionosphere and atmosphere through the space navigation system
