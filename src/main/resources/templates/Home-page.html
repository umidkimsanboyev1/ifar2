Fundamental and applied research
The institute works in the following directions

taking into account the available water resources in the region
conducting medium and long-term research on water use by regions 

reclamation of lands on the basis of geoinformation technologies establishment of a system of assessment of the state, the creation of new ways to increase soil fertility 

Carrying out research on artificial intelligence and "smart technologies", creation of agro-technologies adapted to local soil, crop and agricultural products 

study of plants and their habitats, the optimal level of nutrients in plants, macro and micro elements of the soil on the basis of methods of physicochemical analysis 

Development of research and development on "green economy", renewable energy sources and resource-saving technologies 

In electronics, mechatronics and robotics
development of applied research, processing and analysis of spacecraft data
